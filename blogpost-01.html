A First Time For Everything

Learing to Dive in Aqaba, Jordan

I sudenly found myself with a week off while working in Jordan. After a bit of research I booked myself on the 4 hour bus journey from Amman to Aqaba and signed up for a Padi diving course at a local diving school.

The next morning I turned up for my first lesson. No need for the pool work here. After a thorough briefing it was straight into the red sea for my first ever dive. There was so much to see. Like diving in a huge tropical aquarium. I was hooked.

"Some of the best dives we have done!"
